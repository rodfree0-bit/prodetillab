faq
Quick answers.
Do you need water or power at my location?
No. Our units are 100% self-contained with pressurized water tanks and onboard generators. We need nothing from your property ”” just access to your vehicle.
How long does a service take?
Quick Detail ~1.5 hrs · Deep Interior ~2.5 hrs · Showroom Full 5+ hrs. Your day continues uninterrupted while we work.
Can I book for today?
Yes, subject to availability in your area. Same-day slots are often open. The app shows real-time availability.
Are you insured?
Yes. Every technician carries $1M general liability insurance. Your vehicle is fully covered throughout the service.
How does payment work?
100% in-app via Stripe. We accept all major cards, Apple Pay and Google Pay. No cash accepted.
What if it rains on my booking day?
For heavy rain we'll contact you to reschedule at no charge. Light rain is fine for interior services. We'll always communicate proactively.
